EPR Tool Employee Performance Reporting Tool

EPR Tool is a corporate reporting platform handcrafted and tailored for Octopus Digital's reporting requirements. It converts operational Jira records into leadership-ready planning, performance, and delivery intelligence.

business_center Octopus Digital Reporting Framework

Leadership Value

  • Move from activity tracking to decision intelligence: leadership sees delivery confidence, financial posture, and execution risk in one narrative instead of fragmented Jira views.
  • Act before failure, not after escalation: EPR surfaces pre-failure signals, hidden slippage patterns, and capacity pressure early enough for corrective action.
  • Challenge assumptions with evidence: plan versions, budget movement, leave-adjusted productivity, and phase diagnostics make trade-off decisions auditable and defensible.
  • Run governance reviews at strategic altitude: discussions shift from ticket-level noise to outcomes, intervention options, and expected business impact.
